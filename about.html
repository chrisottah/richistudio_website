The
Story
Behind
the Lens

I've been photographing since 2015, but for me, this has never been just about taking pictures. It's about observing energy. Light on skin. Movement in a room. The pause between moments that most people miss.

My work lives in that in-between space — where bold meets calm, where direction meets spontaneity.

"When the camera disappears and what's left is honesty."

In lifestyle photography, I look for rhythm and connection. In events, I chase rare moments — the blur of motion, the exchange of glances, the electricity in the air. In beauty, I lean into detail and form, sculpting light to celebrate confidence, individuality, and strength.

I don't overcomplicate shoots. I create space. I guide softly. I let people settle into themselves. That's when the magic happens.

Being based in the North East of England influences my work more than I probably realise — open coastlines, industrial textures, shifting skies. There's grit and softness here, and I carry both into what I create.

If you're drawn to imagery that feels expressive, a little cinematic, and entirely authentic — let's create something powerful together.

The Philosophy

What drives every frame

Authenticity Over Performance
